Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024
Effective Date: 2024

PDF Manager ("we", "us", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: All your PDF files and documents are stored locally on your device. We do not access, transmit, or store your files on our servers. Your privacy is our top priority.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect minimal information necessary for the App to function:

  • App Preferences: Theme settings (dark mode preference), notification preferences, and other app settings
  • Biometric Lock Settings: Your preference for enabling/disabling biometric authentication
  • Onboarding Status: Whether you have completed the initial onboarding process

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, feature usage, and crash reports (via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics)
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics to improve functionality (via Firebase Performance)

1.3 PDF Files and Documents

Your PDF Files: All PDF files, documents, images, and content you create, process, or store using the App are stored exclusively on your device. We do not:
  • Access your PDF files
  • Transmit your files to our servers
  • Store your files in the cloud
  • Share your files with third parties

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

2.1 App Functionality

  • To provide and maintain the App's features and services
  • To remember your preferences (theme, settings, etc.)
  • To enable biometric authentication when you choose to use it
  • To process PDF operations locally on your device

2.2 App Improvement

  • To analyze app usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To identify and fix bugs and crashes
  • To optimize app performance
  • To develop new features and functionality

2.3 Communication

  • To respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • To send important app updates and notifications (if you opt-in)

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • PDF Files: Stored in your device's internal or external storage
  • App Preferences: Stored using Android DataStore on your device
  • File Metadata: Information about your PDF files (names, sizes, dates) stored locally

3.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local data encryption where applicable
  • Secure biometric authentication using Android's BiometricPrompt API
  • No transmission of sensitive data to external servers
  • Regular security updates and patches
Note: While we implement security measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and any passwords you set for PDF protection.

4. Permissions We Request

The App requires certain permissions to function properly:

Permission Purpose Required
Camera To scan documents and IDs using your device's camera Yes (for scanning features)
Storage/Media To read and save PDF files on your device Yes (for file operations)

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings. However, revoking permissions may limit or disable certain App features.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Firebase Services

We use Google Firebase services for app analytics and crash reporting:

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the App
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports and error logs to help us fix bugs
  • Firebase Performance: Monitors app performance metrics
  • Firebase Remote Config: Allows us to update app configuration remotely

Firebase data collection is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt-out of analytics data collection through your device settings.

5.2 Google Play Store

For Premium subscriptions, we use Google Play Billing, which is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5.3 Open Source Libraries

The App uses open-source libraries including:

  • PDFBox (for PDF processing)
  • Jetpack Compose (UI framework)
  • Material Design Components
  • CameraX (for camera functionality)

These libraries do not collect or transmit your personal data.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

6.1 Service Providers

  • With Firebase (Google) for analytics and crash reporting (anonymous data only)
  • With Google Play Store for subscription processing

6.2 Legal Requirements

  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users

6.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred, but your privacy rights will continue to be protected.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View app preferences and settings within the App
  • Modify Your Data: Change preferences and settings at any time
  • Delete Your Data: Delete PDF files and clear app data through device settings
  • Revoke Permissions: Revoke camera or storage permissions through device settings

7.2 Opt-Out Options

  • Analytics: Opt-out of Firebase Analytics through your device's Google settings
  • Notifications: Disable notifications through App settings or device settings
  • Biometric Lock: Disable biometric authentication through App settings

7.3 Data Deletion

To delete all App data:

  • Delete PDF files through the App's file management features
  • Clear app data through your device's Settings → Apps → PDF Manager → Clear Data
  • Uninstall the App to remove all associated data

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

If you are using the App from outside your country of residence, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in your country of residence or other countries where we operate. By using the App, you consent to such transfer.

10. Data Retention

10.1 App Preferences

We retain your app preferences (theme, settings) until you delete the App or clear app data.

10.2 PDF Files

Your PDF files are stored on your device until you delete them. We do not retain copies of your files.

10.3 Analytics Data

Firebase Analytics data is retained according to Google's data retention policies (typically 14 months).

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Notifying you through the App (for material changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted on this page.

12. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section.

13. GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request restriction of processing your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section.

To help protect your privacy and data:

  • Keep your device and the App updated to the latest version
  • Use strong passwords for PDF protection
  • Enable biometric lock for additional security
  • Regularly back up important PDF files
  • Be cautious when sharing PDF files
  • Review app permissions periodically
